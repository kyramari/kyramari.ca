Hi – nice to meet you – you can call me Kyra.

I am an Ottawa-based Graphic Designer.

Graphic design is literally everywhere you look. It's such a unique way to convey messages that impact people in ways they might not even realize. Being able to fill the world with colour, pattern and imagery and somehow make a living off of it, is my inspiration.

I’ve loved art and craft from a very young age. I’ve enjoyed painting, pottery, drawing, sculpture, doodling and digital design – anything really that involves visual art and working with my hands! I love traditional art just as much as digital and like to use both in my marketing and communications work.
